Our Mission

Uniting All Ohioans
Around a Shared Fight

FORGE America Now is a nonpartisan nonprofit dedicated to exposing data center abuse — holding corporations and government officials accountable and bringing communities together across political lines. We do not endorse candidates. We champion citizens.

⚡
Electric Grid Crisis
One data center company is demanding 800 MW of power — equal to the entire cities of Cleveland or Cincinnati — straining a grid built for families, not hyperscale corporations.
$
$2.5 Billion in Tax Breaks
Since 2017, the data center industry has avoided $2.5 billion in Ohio sales taxes. School districts like Parma are projected to lose $4M in state funding as a direct result.
🛡
Your Data, Your Rights
These facilities hold your health records, banking data, social media activity, and emails. They are building comprehensive profiles on every American — and the build-out is accelerating.
📊
71% of Americans Agree
A May 2026 Gallup poll found 71% of Americans oppose data centers near their communities. This is not foreign propaganda — it is citizens with legitimate, documented grievances.

The Issues

What's Really at Stake for Ohio

⚡
Electric Grid Overload
AEP Ohio warns the grid was never designed for this surge. Data centers consume city-scale electricity, and the rate increases are being passed to Ohio families and businesses — not the corporations causing the strain.
💰
Tax Abatement Abuse
Scioto County: 15-year, 75% tax abatement. New Albany: 15-year, 100% abatement for Microsoft in exchange for just 20 jobs. Schools lose millions while corporations pocket billions in subsidies.
💧
Water Consumption
Mega data centers consume millions of gallons of water daily for cooling. Ohio's EPA is only now being asked to require basic tracking. Our water resources are being privatized without public consent.
🏠
False Jobs Promise
A $420M, 200-acre facility may employ just 100 people. FORGE America Now's assessment: data centers are a net zero for real Ohio economic development. The jobs promise is the industry's biggest lie.
🔒
4th Amendment & Privacy
Your health records, banking data, shopping history, and social media flow through these facilities. These centers enable comprehensive surveillance profiles on every American — with no meaningful oversight.
🚒
Infrastructure Burden
In Jerome Township, data centers required so many fire department inspections that residents faced new service surcharges. Local governments absorb hidden costs; corporations pay nothing extra.
